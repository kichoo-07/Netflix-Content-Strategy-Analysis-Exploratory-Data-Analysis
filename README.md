## 📌 Project Overview
Netflix is the world's leading streaming entertainment service. This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Netflix’s extensive catalog of movies and TV shows to uncover trends in content strategy, regional focus, and genre popularity.

## 🎯 Business Problem
To maintain a competitive edge and reduce subscriber churn, streaming platforms must make data-driven decisions regarding content acquisition and original production. The objective of this analysis is to identify historical trends in Netflix's content library to recommend optimal release strategies and target audience engagement tactics.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Analytical Tools
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **Analytical Concepts:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Cleaning, Handling Missing Values, Univariate & Bivariate Analysis, Data Visualization & Storytelling

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Value
* **Content Ratios:** Analyzed the distribution between Movies and TV Shows to understand Netflix's shifting production focus over the last decade.
* **Global Expansion:** Mapped content availability across different countries to identify primary target markets and regional growth opportunities.
* **Genre & Rating Analysis:** Evaluated the most frequent maturity ratings and popular genres, providing actionable recommendations for future content investments.
* **Release Timing:** Visualized the optimal months and seasons for content releases to maximize viewership engagement.
